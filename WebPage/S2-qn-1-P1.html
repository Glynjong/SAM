This artwork is called We Are Family. It was made by artist Vicente Delgado in 2014. We Are Family was created by joining different soft toys together. The colourful artwork shows how different groups of people in Singapore live in harmony with one another. The soft toys each have a unique shape, a smiley face and are all connected. They feel like members of a family. The artwork shows how every individual is stronger as part of a community than if he or she were alone.
