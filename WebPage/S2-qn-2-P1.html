Mamashop (2012)

Dawn Ng

Archival inkjet print collage, edition 3/3
120 x 160 cm
Singapore Art Museum collection

Dawn Ng was born in Singapore in 1982 and graduated from The Slade School of Fine Art, London and Georgetown University, Washington where she acquired skills in drawing, painting, photography as well as writing.

This artwork is part of a series of hand-crafted, large scale photo collages by artist Dawn Ng that presents a cheeky look at present day Singapore.

It highlights Singapore’s unique characteristics and traits, set against common, everyday landscapes.

Mamashop is a reference to the provision shops at the HDB void decks, where you can buy drinks, candy, chips and even toys!

“EVERYTHING YOU EVER WANTED IS RIGHT HERE”, also tells us how we can easily find our household items that we need, just right at the Mamashop. It displays the ease and convenience of what is close by. It also reminds us to always appreciate each moment, person and object we have right in front of us.
