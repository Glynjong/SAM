What is

Singapore Art Museum?

Singapore Art Museum, also known as SAM, opened its doors in 1996 as the first art museum in Singapore!

SAM focuses on international contemporary art, especially art from Singapore and in Southeast Asia. 

What is

Contemporary Art?

Contemporary art is art made in the present time. Contemporary artists create new artworks that reflect and respond to ideas, issues and events they see around the world.

Contemporary artists also work with a wide range of mediums, from common materials like paint or clay, to more uncommon ones like sugar or even hair! Often, their choice of materials add meaning or reinforces the message the artist wants to convey.
